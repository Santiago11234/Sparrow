ABOUT US

Our mission is to encourage exposure to computer science in not only an engaging but educational way. Through "Sparrow", students are able to learn java through a series of problems ranging from exciting adventures to puzzle through dragging blocks to complete and run pieces of code to solve tasks.
The Java Sparrow is a small, plump finch with a very thick pink bill. The black head and white cheek patch are distinctive.
Help and design the sparrow to navigate your way through obstacles using java!
