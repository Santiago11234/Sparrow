SPARROW

Our mission is to encourage exposure to computer science in not only an engaging but educational way for young children. Through "Sparrow", students are able to learn hands-on beginner programming with curated lessons and puzzles to create code and solve challenges.

The Java Sparrow is a small, plump finch with a distinctive black head and white cheek patch. Sparrows are known to be incredibly intelligent and curious.
Embark on a thrilling journey with a little bird and overcome obstacles with the use of code!
